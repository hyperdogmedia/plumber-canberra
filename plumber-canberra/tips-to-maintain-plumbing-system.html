Ways Professional Plumbers Can Help With a Blocked Drain

When you're dealing with drain blockages, licensed plumbing experts have professional equipment and tools to identify and resolve the problem effectively. As an example, they can use hydro jetting to flush your pipes.

A drain snake is a professional device consisting of a flexible cable with a grabber attachment that can extract hair and other clogs. Most plumbers also use a powered snake for difficult blockages.

Inspection
Clogged pipes can be a serious hassle for any property owner. Apart from being inconvenient and creating bad smells, they can become a health risk if ignored.

Be aware of these warning signs: bad smells, poor drainage, and unusual noises from your pipes.

While you might attempt some simple fixes first, severe blockages need expert assistance. A licensed professional may need to check the street side plug and use a camera to identify the origin of the clog.

Using a Plunger
A plunger (also known as a force cup) is a essential tool for unblocking blocked drains. For optimal effectiveness, first running the plunger under hot water to soften the rubber and create a better seal.

Expert Techniques
For persistent clogs, professional plumbers might use hydro jetting. This professional method uses pressurized water to completely clear your pipes.

Keep in mind that while chemical cleaners are readily available from stores, they can be harsh to your pipes. It's more effective to contact a professional plumber for serious blockages.
