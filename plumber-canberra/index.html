Ways Professional Plumbers Can Help With a Blocked Drain

When you're facing drain blockages, professional plumbers have specialized tools and equipment to identify and resolve the problem properly. As an example, they can use water jetting to clear your pipes.

A drain snake is a professional device consisting of a flexible cable with a hook attachment that can remove buildup and other obstructions. Many plumbers also use a motorized auger for tough blockages.

Inspection
Drain blockages can be a major problem for any resident. In addition to being annoying and producing unpleasant odours, they can become a health risk if not addressed.

Be aware of these telltale signs: foul odours, poor drainage, and unusual noises from your pipes.

While you might consider trying some simple fixes first, major clogs need professional help. A licensed professional may need to check the street side plug and use a CCTV to identify the source of the clog.

Plunger Solutions
A sink plunger (also known as a plumber's friend) is a basic tool for fixing blocked drains. For best results, first running the plunger under hot water to make it more flexible and create a better seal.

Advanced Solutions
For difficult blockages, licensed technicians might use high-pressure water cleaning. This professional method uses pressurized water to effectively clean your plumbing system.

Remember that while chemical cleaners are available from stores, they can be harmful to your pipes. It's safer to contact a professional plumber for major clogs.
