Ways Professional Plumbers Can Help With a Blocked Drain

When you're dealing with clogged pipes, licensed plumbing experts have specialized equipment and tools to identify and resolve the blockage effectively. For instance, they can use water jetting to clear your pipes.

A plumber's snake is a specialized tool consisting of a long cable with a hook attachment that can clear debris and other obstructions. Many plumbers also use a powered snake for stubborn blockages.

Initial Assessment
Drain blockages can be a major problem for any resident. Besides being frustrating and causing odours, they can develop into a health risk if left untreated.

Look for these telltale signs: foul odours, poor drainage, and gurgling sounds from your plumbing.

While you might consider trying some DIY solutions first, major clogs require professional help. A qualified plumber may need to check the street side plug and use a CCTV to find the origin of the clog.

Plunger Solutions
A plunger (also known as a force cup) is a essential tool for unblocking drain blockages. For optimal effectiveness, start by running the plunger under warm water to make it more flexible and create a better seal.

Advanced Solutions
For difficult blockages, licensed technicians might use water jetting. This professional method uses pressurized water to effectively clean your drain lines.

Keep in mind that while chemical cleaners are readily available from shops, they can be harsh to your pipes. It's more effective to call a licensed plumber for major clogs.
