Ways Professional Plumbers Can Help With a Blocked Drain

When you're dealing with clogged pipes, professional plumbers have professional tools and equipment to locate and clear the problem effectively. For example, they can use high-pressure water jetting to clear your pipes.

A plumber's snake is a specialized tool consisting of a long cable with a grabber attachment that can extract debris and other clogs. Most plumbers also use a mechanical auger for stubborn blockages.

Initial Assessment
Drain blockages can be a major problem for any property owner. Apart from being inconvenient and producing unpleasant odours, they can turn into a health hazard if left untreated.

Watch out for these indicators: foul odours, poor drainage, and gurgling sounds from your drains.

While you might consider trying some DIY solutions first, severe blockages need expert assistance. A qualified plumber may need to examine the drain clean-out and use a video inspection to identify the cause of the blockage.

Plunger Solutions
A sink plunger (also known as a force cup) is a essential tool for fixing drain blockages. For best results, first running the plunger under hot water to make it more flexible and improve suction.

Advanced Solutions
For stubborn blockages, licensed technicians might use high-pressure water cleaning. This advanced technique uses high-pressure water to thoroughly clean your plumbing system.

Remember that while drain cleaning products are readily available from stores, they can be damaging to your pipes. It's safer to contact a licensed plumber for persistent problems.
