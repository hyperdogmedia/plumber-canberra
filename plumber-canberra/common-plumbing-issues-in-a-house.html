Ways Professional Plumbers Can Help With a Blocked Drain

When you're dealing with blocked drains, professional plumbers have specialized tools and equipment to find and fix the issue properly. For example, they can use hydro jetting to clear your pipes.

A drain snake is a specialized tool consisting of a flexible cable with a grabber fitting that can clear hair and other obstructions. Most plumbers also use a motorized auger for tough blockages.

Initial Assessment
Clogged pipes can be a real nightmare for any property owner. Apart from being frustrating and causing odours, they can develop into a health risk if ignored.

Be aware of these telltale signs: bad smells, poor drainage, and gurgling sounds from your plumbing.

While you might attempt some simple fixes first, major clogs need expert assistance. A licensed professional may need to examine the street side plug and use a CCTV to identify the source of the blockage.

Plunging Techniques
A sink plunger (also known as a force cup) is a essential tool for clearing drain blockages. For maximum efficiency, start by placing the plunger under hot water to make it more flexible and create a better seal.

Professional Methods
For stubborn blockages, licensed technicians might use high-pressure water cleaning. This advanced technique uses high-pressure water to thoroughly clean your plumbing system.

Keep in mind that while drain cleaning products are available from shops, they can be harsh to your pipes. It's safer to reach out to a licensed plumber for serious blockages.
