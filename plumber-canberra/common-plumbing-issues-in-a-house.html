Clogged drains are an issue that homeowners face regularly. Untreated, a blockage in the pipe can cause mold to grow and leaks, or even water or sewage back-ups at your house, and may cause damage to your structure.

When you're facing blocked drains, licensed plumbing experts have special equipment and tools to locate and clear the problem efficiently. For example, they can use hydro jetting to flush your pipes.

Clogged pipes can be a real nightmare for any property owner. In addition to being annoying and causing odours, they can develop into a safety concern if left untreated.
