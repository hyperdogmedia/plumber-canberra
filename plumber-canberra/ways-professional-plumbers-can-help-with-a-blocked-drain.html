Ways Professional Plumbers Can Help With a Blocked Drain

When you're facing blocked drains, licensed plumbing experts have specialized tools and equipment to locate and clear the issue effectively. For instance, they can use hydro jetting to clear your pipes.

A drain snake is a specialized tool consisting of a flexible cable with a hook fitting that can extract debris and other blockages. Many plumbers also use a powered snake for stubborn blockages.

Professional Evaluation
Clogged pipes can be a real nightmare for any resident. Apart from being annoying and producing unpleasant odours, they can become a safety concern if ignored.

Watch out for these indicators: bad smells, slow-draining water, and gurgling sounds from your pipes.

While you might consider trying some home remedies first, severe blockages require expert assistance. A qualified plumber may need to check the drain clean-out and use a video inspection to identify the cause of the clog.

Using a Plunger
A standard plunger (also known as a force cup) is a simple tool for clearing blocked drains. For optimal effectiveness, first placing the plunger under warm water to make it more flexible and improve suction.

Advanced Solutions
For stubborn blockages, licensed technicians might use water jetting. This professional method uses high-pressure water to effectively clean your drain lines.

Keep in mind that while chemical cleaners are available from shops, they can be harmful to your pipes. It's more effective to reach out to a licensed plumber for major clogs.
